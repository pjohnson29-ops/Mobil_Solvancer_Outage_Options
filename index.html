Multi-Year Value Projection

▼
How to read this section: Each row below shows the total cost and CO₂e your fleet incurs over the time horizon shown (5, 10, 15, 20 years), assuming you repeat the same option each time the oil reaches end-of-life. Mobil Solvancer extends oil life so you avoid oil changes you would otherwise have done, which is where the savings and CO₂e reductions come from.

Life Extension Factor (LEF) used: Oil change = 1.0× baseline; Mobil Solvancer @3% or @5% in-service = 2.0× (doubles oil life); Mobil Solvancer + Oil Change = 1.5×. Base oil life: 8 yrs — adjust in Input Variables. Savings shown are cumulative, not annual.
What this row shows: The dollar value of the avoided CO₂e if you were to monetise it through a carbon credit scheme. It is not guaranteed money in pocket. Eligibility depends on the registry and methodology, and prices vary widely (current ranges: $5–$30/t voluntary, $30–$90/t compliance markets). Reference schemes: Australian ACCU · EU ETS · California CCA · Verra VCS. Treat as a directional indicator only.
